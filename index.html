More for Android

  1. {Encrypt your phone} and choose a 10 digit PIN, to for very strong protection if your phone is lost, stolen, or seized.
  2. {Firefox} syncs your passwords securely, and lets you install privacy-protecting extensions like {Adblock Plus}, {Noscript}, and {HTTPS Everywhere}.
  3. {Orbot} and {Orweb} let you browse the web privately and anonymously with Tor.
  4. For savvy users, {K-9} and {APG} let you send encrypted emails.

More for Iphone

  1. According to Apple’s {Legal Process Guidelines}, Facetime calls and iMessages are end-to-end encrypted. This is significant, but Textsecure and Redphone are more secure and trustworthy
  2. Use a PIN on your iPhone for better legal protection against searches, and some protection against thieves.

More for Mac

  1. Filevault is built-in, and protects you if your Mac is lost, stolen, or sized. {Turn on Filevault.}
  2. {GPG tools} lets you encrypt emails using Apple’s Mail.app

More for Windows and GNU/Linux

  1. {Firefox} syncs your passwords securely, and lets you install privacy-protecting extensions like {Adblock Plus}, {Noscript}, and {HTTPS Everywhere}.
  2. For savvy users, {Thunderbird} and {Enigmail} let you encrypt your emails.
  3. GNU/Linux users, install all these tools from your favorite package manager.
